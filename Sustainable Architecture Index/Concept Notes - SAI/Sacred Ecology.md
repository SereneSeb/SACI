# Sacred Ecology

*Concept Category: Relational Threads (Meta)*

Sacred Ecology describes the worldview that sees ecosystems not just as resources, but as kin, ancestors, and spiritual partners. It informs building practices that integrate reverence, restraint, and ritual into land use and design.
