# Ritual Architecture

*Concept Category: Healing & Meaning*

Ritual Architecture refers to spaces designed or adapted for collective ceremonies, daily practices, or symbolic expressions. These architectures encode cosmology, mark life transitions, and maintain communal rhythm.
