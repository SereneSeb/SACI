# Slow Maintenance

*Concept Category: Collapse, Risk, and Renewal*

Slow Maintenance is the practice of ongoing, incremental care of buildings and systems over time. It resists planned obsolescence by honoring repair, ritual upkeep, and the rhythms of human attention.
