# Relational Infrastructure

*Concept Category: Design Philosophy & Futures*

Relational Infrastructure redefines infrastructure not as neutral systems (roads, pipes) but as social and ecological connectors. It asks: Who does this system serve? Who maintains it? What does it connect?
