# Community-Based Care Models

*Concept Category: Community & Governance*

Community-Based Care Models are systems of support where caregiving emerges from within the community rather than being delivered solely through institutions. These models value relational continuity, local knowledge, and distributed responsibility‚Äîoften in response to systemic gaps or collapse.
