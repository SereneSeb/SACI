### Design Strategy  
- Repurposed fishing boats, houseboats, or custom low-draft platforms  
- Blackboards, shelves, books, and supplies built into compact floating interiors  
- Emphasis on maneuverability, safety, and all-weather access  
- Form follows community rhythm, not architectural protocol

### Ecological Function  
- Zero-land footprint, using existing water networks  
- Salvaged construction materials reduce waste  
- Operate with minimal energy and often integrate solar lighting  
- Support lake-based subsistence without adding ecological strain

### Community Role

- Teachers often live within or adjacent to the lake community  
- Models built on trust, reciprocity, and embodied presence  
- Symbol of care amid systemic abandonment
