### Summary  
Y:Cube is a prefabricated, modular housing system developed by YMCA and Rogers Stirk Harbour + Partners to address transitional housing needs in London.

### Ecological Function  
- High thermal efficiency minimizes energy demand  
- Offsite prefabrication reduces construction waste and site impact  
- Renewable materials used in paneling and structure  
- Reusable modules allow future re-siting or adaptation

---

### Community Role  
- Designed for transitional residents exiting homelessness  
- Offers affordable, stable housing as a social safety net  
- Blends individual dignity with access to community support  
- Helps residents regain independence and stability
