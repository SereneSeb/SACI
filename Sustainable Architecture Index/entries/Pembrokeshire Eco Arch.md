### Summary

### Design Strategy  
- Single-span timber arch provides shelter and interior openness  
- Straw bale walls offer thermal mass and insulation  
- Turf and earth roof integrates the home into the landscape  
- Designed and built using hand tools, informal knowledge, and deep ecological ethics
