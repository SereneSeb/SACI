### Design Strategy  
- Longhouse form symbolizing human anatomy  
- Carvings, woven panels, and spatial rituals encode cosmology

### Ecological Function

- Ventilation and thermal performance based on seasonal wind and sun  
- Grounds support biodiversity, food sharing, and ritual planting  
- Circular use of space‚Äîceremony and sleep within the same structure

### Community Role

- Youth learn genealogy, story, and law through embodiment  
- Inter-iwi gatherings, weddings, tangihanga, and conflict resolution  
- Space of justice, mourning, kinship, and renewal
