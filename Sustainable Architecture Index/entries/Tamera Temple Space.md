### Summary  
The Temple Space at Tamera is the spiritual heart of the ecovillageâ€”built as a site of acoustic, energetic, and communal coherence. It invites silence, vision, and planetary intention.

---

### Design Strategy  
- Uses sacred geometry (circles, spirals, golden ratio) in layout and form  
- Built from local stone by hand, with communal labor and spiritual intent  
- Located between two water retention landscapes for energetic harmony  
- Open-sky design encourages resonance and planetary alignment

---

### Ecological Function  
- Sited within permaculture water landscapes  
- Integrated into the ecological and acoustic flow of the site  
- Gardens and energy lines tie it into the broader healing infrastructure

---

### Community Role  
Used by the Tamera community as a shared spiritual commons. It hosts public and private rituals, collective intention-setting, seasonal festivals, and planetary healing meditations. Its care is distributed among designated guardians and spiritual stewards.

---

### Cultural / Symbolic Layer  
- Houses ceremonies of grief, forgiveness, commitment, and planetary healing  
- Serves as a cosmogram of peace: spatial diagram of shared ethical geometry  
- Intertwines Earth-based ritual with architectural meditation

- Supports time-conscious design through seasonal alignment
