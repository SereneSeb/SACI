### Design Strategy  
- Built from necessity using scavenged materials and local ingenuity  
- Spaces range from shaded porches and alleyways to retrofitted garages  
- Flexibility and responsiveness prioritized over fixed forms  
- Typically open-air, walkable, and co-located with homes or shops

### Ecological Function  
- Reduces need for new manufacturing and shipping  
- Extends life of goods beyond planned obsolescence  
- Minimizes landfill waste and system dependency  
- Regenerates informal economies and knowledge cycles

---

### Community Role  
- Anchors emotional and physical survival during collapse  
- Transfer of tools, skills, and stories sustains social fabric  
- Builds interdependence, trust, and local confidence  
- Offers presence and solidarity in a fragmented state system
