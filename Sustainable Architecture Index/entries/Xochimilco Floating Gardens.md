### Design Strategy  
- Soil islands held by willow roots  
- Organic fertilization via aquatic sediment  
- Canal-based irrigation and access  
- Floating and fixed garden clusters adaptable to hydrological cycles

---

### Ecological Function  
- Supports wetland biodiversity and pollinator networks  
- Filters urban water and stores carbon  
- Buffers urban heat and seasonal flooding  
- Grows food close to dense populations

---

- Cultural education and tourism raise awareness and provide income  
- Offers resistance to land speculation and urban expansion  
- Supports seed banks and food justice campaigns
