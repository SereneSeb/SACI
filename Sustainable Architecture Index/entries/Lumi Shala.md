### Summary

---

### Design Strategy  
- Gridshell bamboo system allows large span with minimal material  
- Open-air plan permits passive cooling and breeze circulation  
- Earth-rendered walls modulate moisture and heat  
- Form choreographed to invite light, stillness, and sound attenuation

---

### Ecological Function  
- Bamboo is rapidly renewable and locally grown, with minimal embodied energy  
- On-site construction reduces transport emissions and waste  
- Passive systems eliminate need for mechanical climate control  
- Entire building is compostable, demountable, and ecologically reversible

---

### Community Role  
- Built by skilled local artisans, supporting regenerative economies  
- Used by both international visitors and local community groups  
- Platform for wellness workshops, cultural exchange, and ecological education  
- Generates cultural capital through spatial generosity

---

- Floor and air invite barefoot movement and breath rhythm  
- Walls breathe. Light pulses. Material listens.
