This operation details the construction of earthbag or sandbag walls using locally sourced rubble and soil mixtures. ## âš™ï¸ Equipment & Action Plan
### Equipment Used
Heavy-duty polypropylene bags, barbed wire, tampers, shovels, water containers, plaster tools, and PPE (gloves, boots, masks). Optional mesh or plaster reinforcement used for external finishing.

### Automated Action Plan (AAP)
1. **Foundation Preparation:** Level ground and lay damp-proof membrane or compacted gravel layer.  
2. **Bag Filling:** Fill bags with mixed rubble and soil; maintain consistent moisture for compaction.  
3. **Stacking:** Lay bags in running bond pattern with barbed wire between each course for tension and interlock.  
4. **Tamping:** Compact each course before adding the next layer.  
5. **Finishing:** Apply plaster or render for weather protection; add buttresses for lateral support.  
6. **Inspection:** Verify plumb, compaction, and alignment before plastering.
