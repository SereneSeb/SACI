## âš™ï¸ Equipment & Action Plan
### Equipment Used
Galvanised steel wire mesh rolls, wire cutters, tie wire, pliers, spacers, hammers, and basic PPE (gloves, boots, helmets). Optional small hoist or pulley system for moving filled cages.

### Automated Action Plan (AAP)
1. **Site Preparation:** Foundation trenches excavated and levelled.  
2. **Cage Assembly:** Mesh panels cut and tied into baskets, reinforced with spacers for rigidity.  
3. **Filling:** Rubble manually placed and compacted in layers to minimise voids.  
4. **Stacking:** Cages aligned and tied together in a staggered pattern for wall stability.  
5. **Finishing:** External surfaces optionally plastered or covered with geotextile for weather resistance.  
6. **Inspection:** Wall checked for vertical alignment, tie integrity, and base settlement before approval.
