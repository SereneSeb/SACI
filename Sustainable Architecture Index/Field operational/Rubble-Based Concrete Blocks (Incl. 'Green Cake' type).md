### Automated Action Plan (AAP)
1. **Preparation:** Rubble crushed to <15mm, fines separated, and mixed with limited cement and fly ash (or coal ash if available).  
2. **Mixing:** Dry materials blended thoroughly before adding minimal water for workable consistency.  
3. **Casting:** Mix placed into moulds, compacted manually or by press, then de-moulded carefully.  
4. **Curing:** Blocks covered with damp cloths or misted daily for 7â€“10 days.  
5. **Quality Checks:** Random samples tested for strength, density, and uniformity before deployment.
