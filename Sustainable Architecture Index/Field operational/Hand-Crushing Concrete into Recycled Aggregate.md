## ğŸ” Observations & Findings
Material quality was generally high, with 80% of recovered concrete suitable for reuse. Manual crushing achieved an output rate of approximately 0.8 tonnes/day with eight workers. Dust levels were significant but manageable with masks and spray control. Fine aggregates showed slightly higher contamination rates due to soil mixing near collection sites. Consistent manual effort produced a stable grading curve suitable for lean concrete and paving.

### Safety & Ethics
- Mandatory PPE for all participants  
- Rotational work schedule to avoid fatigue and repetitive strain injuries  
- Controlled water misting to suppress dust and maintain respiratory safety  
- Clear separation of working and residential zones to minimise community exposure
