### Equipment Used
Protective gloves, goggles, crowbars, hammers, hacksaws, manual bending/straightening jigs, hand torches (for cutting where safe), and scales for weighing recovered material.

### Automated Action Plan (AAP)
Work areas were divided into quadrants, prioritised based on visible steel exposure. Teams extracted rebar using hand tools, avoiding thermal cutting in structurally unstable zones. Straightening was performed using jig stations constructed from local materials. A mobile log was maintained to track recovered quantities, corrosion levels, and grade classifications.
