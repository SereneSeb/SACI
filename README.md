Sustainable Architecture Index (SAI)

Overview

The SAI is an open framework for assessing how architecture supports ecological balance, social cohesion, and cultural meaning.
It expands sustainability beyond energy and cost to include the symbolic and relational life of a place.

⸻

Goals
	•	Link environmental data with social and cultural indicators.
	•	Support communities designing for care, cooperation, and longevity.
	•	Provide an open space for shared tools, datasets, and ideas.
